Historic charm meets modern living at J.M. Lofts, a brand new upscale apartment community located in the heart of downtown Haverhill. Originally built in 1882, this one-time shoe factory has been completely renovated into 18 beautifully designed one- and two-bedroom apartment homes. Each modern unit offers an open floor plan with high ceilings, and exposed brick and beams.

Situated on vibrant Washington Street, J.M. Lofts is just steps from independent restaurants, boutique shopping and Haverhill’s Waterfront and Arts Districts. J.M. Lofts is just a minute walk to the MBTA Commuter Rail that takes you into Boston’s North Station in 45 minutes as well as a quick drive to Interstates 495, 93 and 95 for those who are commuting for work or for play. The community is pet-friendly and provides 24-hour on-call maintenance. Live original. Live at J.M. Lofts.
